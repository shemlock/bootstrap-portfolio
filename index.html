Meal Mate is a meal planning and organisation app. Users can view recipes for breakfast, lunch and dinner, add meals to a macro tracker or a weekly meal planner and add notes to a food journal.

Movie Tracker

Movie Tracker is a comprehensive and user-friendly movie searching website that caters to both casual moviegoers and avid film fanatics. Key Features include - Movie Search: search for movies by title,modal popup when movie poster is clicked, trailer embedded in modal , Actor Search: actor's profile: a short overview, theirpicture and 3 highest rated movies , Watchlist Feature: users to create and manage a personalized watchlist , User-Friendly Interface: clean and intuitive design,responsive search bar and easy to find watchlist button

Daily Planner

Daily Planner is a simple calendar application that allows users to add/save tasks, notes and events for each hour of the workday. The current date is displayed at the top and updates automatically. Hours are colour coded: hours past are grey, current hour is red and future hours are green.
